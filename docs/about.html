Research interests

My research focuses in using computational approaches to better understand the role of the gut microbiome in human health. My three main research interests are:

  • Gut microbiome modulation of gut barrier integrity and its effects on host health
  • Expanding our understanding of the gut microbiome from underrepresented communities
  • Probiotic development
